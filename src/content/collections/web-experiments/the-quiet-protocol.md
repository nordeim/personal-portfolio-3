An experiment in subtraction. A reading interface that removes everything
except the reading.

## What was removed

- Likes, shares, metrics
- Notifications, badges, counters
- Recommendations, autoplay, infinite scroll
- Color (entirely)
- Most of the navigation

## What remains

- The text
- The page number
- A single rule: turn the page when you are ready
