Marginalia is a browser extension that lets you mark up the web the way you
mark up a book. Notes, highlights, and a slow, deliberate way of reading.

## Why

Because most reading tools optimize for capture, not for return. Marginalia is
designed to bring you back to the same passage, in the same light, a year
later.
