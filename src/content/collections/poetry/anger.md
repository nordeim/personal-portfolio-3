---
title: Anger
description: A poem that frames anger as storm, destruction, revenge, and regret.
status: Poem
---

Anger is as dreadful as a raging storm
With power known to no one born
It's as murderous as the earthquake below
Striking down one's enemies and foes

It blazes within one's inner soul
Wiping out the conscience deep within
It undoubtedly leads to death and sin
Preparing the way to Hell below

Listening to the devil's beckoning call
To take revenge once and for all
Brings us only to a fate unknown
Which only God would know

A smile creeps up Hade's face
As he welcomes us with eyes ablaze
While we shriek in pain ever so loudly
We then regret for getting so angry
