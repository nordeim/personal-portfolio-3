---
title: Depression
description: A poem about despair, isolation, and the way sadness can drain a life of colour.
status: Poem
---

Depression sucks out the happiness from your soul,
Overwhelming you with an indescribable sorrow.
Despair will engulf you where your heart sits,
Extinguishing any hope which still remains alit.

Your heart will shatter into tiny smithereens,
Your head forever downwards leaning.
Your mouth will become a capsized "U",
Your life fated too to cry the blues.

The spirit and joy in your eyes will be drained,
Becoming as lifeless as the falling rain.
Others will stay far, far away,
As far away as night is from day.

The utter misery will be just too unbearable,
You'll wish for death ever so horrible,
Just to get back the days of joy,
Which you'll never find till the day you die.
