This piece reimagines the fire at Miss Maudie Atkinson's house as an article from The Maycomb Tribune.

The accompanying rationale frames the article as a factual recount intended to show that the fire is as significant and impressionable as the trial of Tom Robinson. It uses a newspaper format, chronological reporting, selected details from the novel, and quoted voices from Maycomb to make the scene feel immediate and public.

The PDF preserves the article and rationale together as a standalone reading document.
